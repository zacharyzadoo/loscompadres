About Us

Our story begins in a tortilla factory created by Ignacio in Hayward, CA. 50 years later his children Ignacio Jr. and Sandra are still proudly serving carnitas and El Max burritos among other town favorites. Each day, food is prepared from scratch with the same family recipe our compadres have enjoyed all these decades. Come visit our family and try our authentic Mexican delicacies.
